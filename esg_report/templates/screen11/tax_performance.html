11.4.3. Tax Governance and Risk Management (GRI 207-2-a, b, c)

We have implemented comprehensive tax governance frameworks to manage tax risks and ensure compliance. Our tax governance mechanisms include regular audits, risk assessments, and transparent reporting.

11.4.4. Stakeholder Engagement and Management of Concerns Related to Tax (GRI 207-3-a)

We maintain an open dialogue with stakeholders regarding our tax strategy and practices. By engaging with stakeholders, we ensure that concerns are addressed, and we provide detailed reports on our tax contributions and policies.
